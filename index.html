About the Author

Words written from lived experience.

E. Wise is an author, speaker, and longtime advocate for emotional recovery. His work draws from more than a decade of quiet practice — guiding individuals through the patient, deliberate work of healing what most people never speak about.

His first book and accompanying course reached readers in over twenty countries, offering a steady voice to people rebuilding after grief, heartbreak, and self-doubt.

"We do not rise by forgetting the fire. We rise because we learned how to carry it."

Rising from the Ashes is his most personal work to date — a companion for anyone ready to meet their pain honestly and begin again.

— E. Wise
