About the Author

Words written from lived experience.

E. Wise is an author, speaker, and longtime advocate for emotional recovery. His work draws from more than a decade of quiet practice — guiding individuals through the patient, deliberate work of healing what most people never speak about.

His first book and accompanying course reached readers in over twenty countries, offering a steady voice to people rebuilding after grief, heartbreak, and self-doubt.

"We do not rise by forgetting the fire. We rise because we learned how to carry it."

Rising from the Ashes is his most personal work to date — a companion for anyone ready to meet their pain honestly and begin again.

— E. Wise
Inside the Book

Eight chapters for the long work ahead.

Each chapter offers reflection, research, and gentle practice — designed to be read slowly, returned to often, and underlined generously.

i.

The Weight We Learn to Carry

Understanding the origins of emotional wounds, and why most of us never named them.

ii.

Naming the Fire

A framework for identifying the specific pain beneath the surface of daily life.

iii.

Stillness as Medicine

Why pause is not avoidance — and how silence becomes part of the healing.

iv.

The Long Walk Through Grief

On the non-linear nature of loss, and the gentle permissions required.

v.

Rebuilding the Inner Voice

Practices for softening self-criticism and restoring self-trust over time.

vi.

Relationships After the Storm

How healing reshapes the way we show up for the people we love.

vii.

A Life Worth Returning To

Finding meaning, ritual, and joy in a life that feels, finally, like your own.

viii.

On Rising

A closing reflection on what it means to stand again — changed, but whole.
